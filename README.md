## M3: Blood Pressure Measurement: A Novel Approach. 

All code used for the Doppler ultrasound sub-system as part of the technical detailed design report for GDBP.  

The measurement of blood pressure serves as not just a fundamental vital sign but also as a functional diagnostic and prognostic marker for a range of diseases. However, measurement of both the systolic and diastolic pressures using a non-invasive device remains challenging. 

Recently, the Royal United Hospital secured a UK patent for a novel measurement technique that combines doppler ultrasound detection of blood flow with novel pulsatile waveforms for occluding the artery that promises to deliver best-in-class measurement of blood pressure. 

This project aims to demonstrate proof-of-concept by developing a commercial prototype of the device.
